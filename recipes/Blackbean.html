Black Bean Breakfast Bowl.

Black Bean Breakfast Bowl.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 avocado, peeled and sliced

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Pour eggs into skillet; cook and stir eggs until set, 3 to 5 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    Place black beans in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave at High power until warm, about 1 minute. Divide between two bowls.

  3. Step 3

    Divide scrambled eggs between each bowl; top each with avocado and salsa, then season with salt and black pepper.
