Layers of lasagna noodles, ground beef, marinara sauce, and creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, topped with mozzarella and baked until golden.

Layers of lasagna noodles, ground beef, marinara sauce, and creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, topped with mozzarella and baked until golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (12 sheets)
  • Ground beef (1 lb / 450g)
  • Onion (1, chopped)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook briefly.
  4. Add ground beef, season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning, and cook until browned.
